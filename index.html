Our Commitment

Committed to quality & service

Kenya Fresh Produce Exporters LTD — sourcing, processing and delivering the finest farm produce from Kenya to the world since 2006.

Incorporated under the Kenyan Companies Act 2005, we source and export a wide range of fresh vegetables, fruits and cut flowers from Kenya. We engage directly with farmers to ensure quality at every step of the supply chain.

We engage in the sourcing, processing and marketing of avocados, sisal, blueberries, sesame, pineapple, macadamia, tea, capsicum and chilli — offering the best value while meeting international export standards.
