Who we are

Committed to quality service & produce

Kenya Fresh Produce Exporters LTD is a limited liability company, incorporated under the Kenyan companies act 2005 and began operation in 2006. We source and export a wide range of fresh vegetables, fruits and cut flowers from Kenya.

We engage in the sourcing, processing and marketing of avocados, sisal, blueberries, sesame, pineapple, macadamia, tea, capsicum and chilli. We offer fresh fruits and vegetables at the best value price. We meet regularly with our farmers and stakeholders in the market to keep up to date on price and supply.

Our supply chain is optimized for our clients. We see to it that fruits and vegetables have timely movement from harvest to exportation. We pride ourselves on supplying the finest fruits and vegetables and providing unparalled customer service.

Why Us

Some of our many goals are; conserving energy and resources, waste reduction, recycling, and use of renewed products. We are dedicated to fostering good relationships with our employees, business partners and local community. We continually strive to maintain the highest standards for a first class food safety program. Quality and customer satisfaction are out top priorities.
