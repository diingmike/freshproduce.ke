Who we are

Kenya Fresh Produce Exporters LTD is a limited liability company, incorporated under the Kenyan companies act 2005 and began operation in 2006. We source and export a wide a wide range of fresh vegetables, fruits and cut flowers from Kenya.

We engage in the sourcing, processing and marketing of avocados, sisal, blueberries, sesame, pineapple, macadamia, tea, capsicum and chilli
